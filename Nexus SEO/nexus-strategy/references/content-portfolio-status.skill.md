# content-portfolio-status.skill

**Role:** Aggregate all published content for a brand into a portfolio view: ranking status, decay signals, last-audit dates, next recommended actions per piece. Solves the "I have hundreds of articles, what do I update?" problem.

**Loaded by:** `nexus-strategy` SKILL.md in mode: content_portfolio_status

---

## Why this skill exists

Content portfolios decay silently. Articles that ranked well 6 months ago lose position. Pieces with stale stats keep generating impressions but conversions drop. Without a portfolio view, content owners don't know what to update or when.

This skill provides the missing dashboard: every piece of content with its current health and next action.

---

## Inputs

Required:
- Content inventory from content-memory (all published content with metadata)

Optional but high-value:
- GSC export (queries × pages — to identify ranking changes per page)
- PageSpeed reports (per-URL — for tech health)
- Decay maps (from decay-mapping in nexus-content — review triggers per piece)
- Audit reports (from nexus-audit — quality scores per piece)

---

## Process

### Step 1 — Load all published content

From content-memory, load every published content piece with:
- URL
- Title
- Publish date
- Last updated date
- Topic / cluster
- Target keyword(s)
- Audit history (when last audited, scores)
- Decay tags (if generated by decay-mapping)

If content-memory is empty: tell user "No published content tracked yet. Add content via [memory loading method] before running portfolio status."

### Step 2 — Cross-reference with GSC (if provided)

For each URL in inventory:
- Match against GSC pages report
- Get current position for primary keyword
- Get position trend (last 90 days vs prior 90 days, if both periods provided)
- Get impressions trend
- Get clicks trend
- Get CTR trend

Categorize:
- **STABLE_TOP** — page ranking 1-3, no decline
- **STABLE_PAGE_1** — page ranking 4-10, no decline
- **DECLINING** — position dropped by 3+ over period
- **PAGE_2** — position 11-20, recovery candidate
- **NOT_RANKING** — no significant impressions

### Step 3 — Cross-reference with decay maps (if available)

For each URL with decay tags:
- Check next-review-by date
- Categorize:
  - **REVIEW_OVERDUE** — review date passed
  - **REVIEW_DUE_SOON** — within 30 days
  - **REVIEW_FUTURE** — beyond 30 days

### Step 4 — Cross-reference with audit history

For each URL with audit history:
- Last audit date
- Last audit score
- Categorize:
  - **NEVER_AUDITED**
  - **AUDIT_OLD** — audited >180 days ago
  - **AUDIT_RECENT** — audited <180 days ago

### Step 5 — Determine next recommended action per piece

For each URL, combine signals to recommend action:

| Ranking | Decay | Audit | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| STABLE_TOP | REVIEW_FUTURE | AUDIT_RECENT | NO_ACTION (don't disrupt ranking content) |
| STABLE_TOP | REVIEW_OVERDUE | * | MINOR_REFRESH (per ranking-status-check PROTECT mode — pricing/stats only) |
| STABLE_TOP | * | NEVER_AUDITED | AUDIT_NOW (baseline scoring needed) |
| STABLE_PAGE_1 | * | * | STANDARD_REVIEW (per SHIELD mode) |
| DECLINING | * | * | URGENT_AUDIT + DIAGNOSE (use nexus-audit + nexus-tech-seo) |
| PAGE_2 | * | * | OPTIMIZATION_CANDIDATE (per RECOVERY mode) |
| NOT_RANKING | * | NEVER_AUDITED | DIAGNOSE_FAILURE (why isn't this ranking?) |
| NOT_RANKING | * | AUDIT_RECENT | CONSIDER_DEPRECATION (audit was recent, still not ranking, may not be salvageable) |

### Step 6 — Aggregate portfolio metrics

- Total content pieces: [N]
- Distribution by status: [breakdown]
- Total NEEDS_ACTION: [N]
- Total NO_ACTION_NEEDED: [N]
- Highest-priority actions (top 10): [ranked list]

### Step 7 — Calendar projection

Generate a maintenance calendar:
- This month: [N pieces need action]
- Next month: [N pieces will need action based on decay schedules]
- Next 90 days: [N pieces]
- Next 6 months: [N pieces]

## Behaviors

1. **Default to NO_ACTION for performing content.** STABLE_TOP content shouldn't be touched unnecessarily — protect ranking.

2. **Surface declining content urgently.** Position drops are time-sensitive — every week matters.

3. **Don't recommend deprecation lightly.** A page that's never ranked might still be early in its lifecycle. Only recommend deprecation after multiple audits + sustained non-ranking.

4. **Calendar projection is the user value.** "What do I need to do this month?" is the question. Answer it specifically.

5. **Cross-reference with decay maps.** Decay-tagged content has explicit review schedules. Honor them.

6. **Portfolio health score motivates maintenance.** Single number captures portfolio state — useful for tracking improvement over time.

7. **Ranking-status-aware.** Recommendations integrate with ranking protection system (PROTECT/SHIELD/RECOVERY/FULL modes from nexus-content).
